Doors

A vehicle typically has two types of doors: front doors and rear doors. Loosely related are: vehicle hoods and vehicle trunk lids. There are also doors known as a "hatch" (see "door categorization" below). Conventional, scissor, butterfly, suicide, sliding, gull-wing and canopy doors are some of the types discussed along with descriptions of each
What is the door frame of a car? Doorframes perform many vital roles, particularly involving door rigidity, wind noise reduction while driving and keeping doors watertight. In addition, as an integral component of vehicle bodies, doorframes are an important part of the overall strength of a car body.
