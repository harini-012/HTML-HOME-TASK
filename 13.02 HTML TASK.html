ABOUT US

The Hindu is an English-language newspaper in India that was founded in 1878. It is considered one of the most influential newspapers in the country. History: The Hindu was founded as a weekly publication by the Triplicane Six. It became a daily in 1889. The newspaper has been family-owned since 1905, when S. Kasturi Ranga Iyengar bought it from the original founders. The Hindu is now owned by Iyengar's descendants, the "Kasturi family". Features: The Hindu is known for its balanced and reliable news coverage. It is known for its strong reporting from the national capital and southern states. The Hindu has a large network of correspondents and photographers across India.
