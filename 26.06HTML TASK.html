Instructions Of examination results

Result Review

  • Heads of Departments (HODs) and the second senior faculty member review the results within their respective departments.
  • This review includes analyzing subject-wise mark reports (both End Semester Examination (ESE) and total marks - ESE+CIA).
  • Moderation of marks may be requested if needed.
  • The Principal and Controller of Examinations (COE) scrutinize the review committee's recommendations.


Important points

  • The college website for result publication is www.sdnbvc.com.
  • Continuous Assessment (CA) marks are crucial and must be submitted on time.
  • For certain students (hearing and speaking impaired and special needs), exemptions may be granted from specific language and elective courses.
  • Malpractice during examinations can lead to cancellation of the exam and a withheld result
